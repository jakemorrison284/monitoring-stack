# Alert Consolidation Plan

## Overview
This document outlines the plan for consolidating duplicate alerts and implementing clear thresholds for critical alerts.

## Action Plan
1. **Consolidate Duplicate Alerts**: Identify and merge alerts that monitor the same conditions to reduce noise. This will involve reviewing existing alert configurations and determining overlaps.
2. **Define Clear Thresholds**: For critical alerts, establish specific thresholds based on historical data and team feedback. This will ensure timely responses without overwhelming the team.
3. **Regular Review Cycle**: Establish a bi-annual review process for alert configurations to ensure they remain relevant and effective.
4. **Stakeholder Engagement**: Share findings with the payment processing team and gather feedback to refine alerting strategy.
5. **Data Analysis**: Conduct a historical data analysis to fine-tune thresholds and response actions for alerts, minimizing false positives and ensuring timely incident detection.

## Conclusion
Implementing this action plan will help streamline alerting processes, reduce noise, and enhance response effectiveness within the monitoring stack.
